# ThinkPoint Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** December 17, 2025

## 1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the owners of the site.

> **Summary:** Cookies are digital notes we save on your browser to remember your preferences and keep you logged in.

## 2. How We Use Cookies
We use cookies for the following purposes:

*   **Essential Cookies:** These are necessary for the website to function (e.g., logging into the Copoint dashboard). You cannot switch these off.
*   **Performance & Analytics:** We use these to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site. Data is aggregated and anonymous.
*   **Functional Cookies:** These enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your language preference or region.

## 3. Third-Party Cookies
In some cases, we use trusted third-party services that may also set cookies on your device:
*   **Google Analytics:** To help us understand how you use the site.
*   **Stripe:** To process payments securely and detect fraud.

## 4. Managing Your Preferences
Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings. You can choose to block all cookies, but this may cause parts of the thinkpoint.ee website to stop working effectively.

For more information about how to manage cookies, visit [aboutcookies.org](https://www.aboutcookies.org).

## 5. Updates to This Policy
We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information on our privacy practices.
